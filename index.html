How Many Slices On A Pizza

Most pizza chains and restaurants will slice a pizza tray depending on the size, here is the common metric: 6 slices per small pizza, 8 slices per medium pizza, 10 slices on a large pizza.

How Many Slices do we eat on average?

On average...

A child eats 2 small pizza slices or 1.5 medium pizza slices or 1 large pizza slice.

An adult (average eater) eats 4 small pizza slices or 3 medium pizza slices or 2 large pizza slices.

A pizza lover (eats more than average) eats 6 small pizza slices or 4.5 medium pizza slices or 3 large pizza slices.
